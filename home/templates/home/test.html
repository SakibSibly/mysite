Atish Kumar Dipongkor

Publications | Experience | Awards | Blog | CV

Atish Kumar Dipongkor, a computer science PhD student at George Mason University (GMU) in the United States, has a educational background in software engineering, with a B.Sc. and M.Sc. from the University of Dhaka (DU) in Bangladesh. He has more than 7 years of experience in software development and research. Currently, he is working at SAGE lab, directed by Dr. Kevin Moran. His research focus is on the intersection between software engineering and machine learning, as well as natural language processing.
